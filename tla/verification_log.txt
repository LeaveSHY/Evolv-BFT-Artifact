INTERPRETATION FOR ARTIFACT EVALUATION
==============================================================================
1. Safety properties (Theorems 1-3 in paper) are verified:
   - Exhaustively on component specs (Safety, MultiLeader, Reconfiguration)
   - Via deep partial exploration on composed specs (billions of states, zero violations)

2. Liveness (Theorem 2) requires partial synchrony (GST), which is a
   standard assumption not encodable as a TLA+ fairness constraint without
   additional timing oracle. The Reconfiguration spec verifies liveness
   under its specific fairness conditions.

3. The TLA+ specifications directly correspond to paper formalizations:
   - OctopusSafety → Theorem 1 (Agreement + Commit Safety)
   - OctopusMultiLeader → Theorem 3 (Global Ordering via GBC)
   - OctopusReconfiguration → Theorem 2 (Safe Reconfiguration)
   - OctopusByzantine → Safety under Byzantine adversary (f < n/3)
   - OctopusGBC → GBC properties G1-G4 (Section III-C)
   - OctopusComposed → Full system safety composition
